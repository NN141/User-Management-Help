View Custom Reports

This section describes:

  • How to export the reports to MS Excel
  • How to view pre-designed reports
  • How to get the reports e-mailed to you
  1. On the User Management Home page, click the Settings icon  and select Custom Reports under Report. Reports page will appear.

    2. Export the Reports to MS Excel;

  1. Click the Export to Excel icon  to export the report data to MS-Excel.
  2. Download Report link will appear to download the report, after export is complete.

   3. View the pre-designed Report;

  1. To view a report, select the required report name and click on it.A dialog box will appear.
  2. Click Generate Report to generate the respective report.

  4. To get the reports e-mailed to you;

  1. Select the required report name and click on it.A dialog box will appear.
  2. Click Have this report mailed to you link to get the respective report emailed to your email account.
  3. Select the frequency, starting on date and the Covering.
  4. Click “Save Settings” to save the configured settings.

If you have already configured e-mail settings for similar reports, it will be displayed on the window.

Required Settings to get reports e-mailed to you

Frequency

This determines how often the reports should be mailed to the user

Starting On

This shows which date the first report should be mailed on

Covering

The date range that the report will cover

 

To find the reports those are scheduled for delivery via e-mail, click the link “View your reports”.
