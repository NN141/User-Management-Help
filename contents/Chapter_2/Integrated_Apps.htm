Other applications that are integrated with User Management app can be seen on the Side bar to the right side when respective User’s detailed page is opened.

 

The links to the other related applications are displayed when the respective users are associated with the other applications.

 

This feature is helpful as it allows viewing related information in other applications.
e.g.  Call History under Call Center.
